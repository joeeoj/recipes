# Instant Pot Pulled Pork

Pretty good

## info

* 1 hour 30 minutes
* 6-8 servings

## ingredients

Dry rub

* 3 tbsp light brown sugar
* 2 tsp kosher salt
* 1 tsp ground mustard
* 1 tsp black pepper
* 1 tsp onion powder
* 1 tsp paprika
* 1/2 tsp garlic powder
* 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper

Pork and liquid

* 4 lb pork butt (or shoulder) roast (boneless or bone-in)
* 2 tbsp olive oil
* 1.5 cups chicken broth
* 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
* 1 tsp liquid smoke
* 1/2 to 1 cup barbecue sauce (your preferred brand)

## steps

Prep

1. Trim excess fat from the pork roast
2. Cut roast into 4 equal chunks
3. Combine all dry rub ingredients (brown sugar, salt, ground mustard, black pepper, onion powder, paprika, garlic powder, cayenne pepper) in a bowl and whisk together
4. Coat pork chunks thoroughly with the dry rub mixture

Cook

1. Turn instant pot to saute mode and heat olive oil
2. Sear 2 pork pieces for approximately 2 minutes per side
3. Remove seared pork and set aside
4. Sear remaining 2 pork pieces for approximately 2 minutes per side
5. Remove and set aside with other pork
6. Press cancel to turn off saute mode
7. Add 1/2 cup chicken broth to the pot
8. Deglaze the pot bottom using a wooden spoon, scraping up all browned bits
9. Add remaining chicken broth, Worcestershire sauce, and liquid smoke
10. Place all pork chunks in the liquid, spacing evenly
11. Seal the lid and set vent to sealing position
12. Pressure cook **high 60 minutes**
13. **Natural release 20 minutes**
14. Transfer pork to a bowl
15. Shred pork with two forks
16. Mix in barbecue sauce (or use the cooking liquid if preferred)
