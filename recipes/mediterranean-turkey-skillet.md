# Mediterranean Turkey Skillet

One (large) skillet. Good for weeknights.

## info

* 20 minutes
* 4 servings

## ingredients

* 1 tbsp olive oil
* 1 small yellow onion
* 3 cloves garlic
* 1 lb ground turkey (93% lean)
* 1 medium zucchini
* 1 can chickpeas (15 oz, drained)
* 1 can diced tomatoes (14 oz)
* 1.5 cups frozen/precooked brown rice
* 2 tsp Mediterranean or Italian seasoning
* 1/4 cup water or broth

more ingredients you might already have but should check

* kosher salt
* black pepper

toppings

* 1/3 cup feta cheese crumbles
* 1/4 cup pistachios, chopped
* 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
* lemon wedges

## steps

Prep

1. Dice onion
2. Mince garlic
3. Dice zucchini
4. Drain chickpeas
5. Chop pistachios
6. Chop parsley

Cook

1. Heat olive oil in large skillet with lid over medium heat
2. Add onion and garlic, cook for 3-4 minutes, stirring occasionally
3. Add ground turkey, breaking apart with wooden spoon until cooked through
4. Add zucchini and cook for 3-4 minutes, stirring occasionally, until softened
5. Stir in chickpeas, diced tomatoes, rice, 1 tsp salt, 1/2 tsp pepper, and Mediterranean seasoning
6. Pour 1/4 cup water or broth over mixture
7. Cover and simmer on medium-low for 10 minutes
8. If excess liquid remains, uncover, increase heat to medium-high, and stir until liquid evaporates
9. Adjust seasoning to taste
10. Top with feta, pistachios, parsley, and lemon wedges

## notes

* swap vegetables, toppings, or seasonings to preference
