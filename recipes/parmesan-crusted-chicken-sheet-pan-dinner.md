# Parmesan Crusted Chicken Sheet Pan Dinner

Dinner on one pan with parmesan crusted chicken, roasted potatoes, and green beans

## info

* 60 minutes (15 min prep, 40 min cook)
* 4 servings

## ingredients

Chicken

* 1.5 lbs chicken breast (about 3-4 pieces, approximately 1 inch thick)
* 2 tbsp olive oil
* 2 cloves garlic, minced
* 1/3 cup grated parmesan
* 1/3 cup breadcrumbs (Italian, plain, or panko)
* 1/2 tsp cracked pepper
* 1/2-3/4 tsp sea salt

Potatoes

* 2 lbs red potatoes (cut into bite-sized pieces)
* 2 cloves garlic, minced
* 2 tbsp olive oil
* 2 tbsp grated parmesan
* salt and pepper to taste

Green Beans

* 1 lb fresh green beans (ends trimmed)
* 1 tbsp olive oil
* 1 clove garlic, minced
* 1 tbsp parmesan (optional)
* salt and pepper to taste

## steps

Prep

1. Preheat oven to 425 F
2. Mince all garlic cloves (5-ish for all components)
3. Cut red potatoes into bite-sized pieces
4. Trim ends from green beans
5. Line a sheet pan with parchment paper

Cook

1. Toss potato pieces with 2 tbsp olive oil, 1-2 cloves minced garlic, 2 tbsp parmesan, salt and pepper
2. Spread potatoes on one-third of the prepared sheet pan
3. Bake potatoes for 10-15 minutes
4. While potatoes cook, coat chicken pieces with 2 tbsp olive oil, 2 cloves minced garlic, 1/3 cup parmesan, 1/3 cup breadcrumbs, 1/2 tsp pepper, and 1 tsp salt
5. Remove pan from oven and add coated chicken pieces to the pan
6. Toss green beans with 1 tbsp olive oil, 1 clove minced garlic, optional 1 tbsp parmesan, salt and pepper
7. Add green bean mixture to the remaining space on the sheet pan
8. Bake approximately 25 minutes until chicken reaches 165 F internal temperature
9. Optional: broil on high for 4-5 minutes for extra crispy potatoes
10. Cool several minutes before serving

## notes

* Easy cleanup with everything on one pan
* Use a meat thermometer to ensure chicken reaches safe internal temperature of 165 F
* Can adjust seasoning amounts to taste
