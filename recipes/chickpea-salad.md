# Favorite Chickpea Salad

it good

## info

* 20 minutes
* 4-6 servings

## ingredients

* 2 cans (15 oz each) chickpeas, rinsed and drained (or 3 cups cooked chickpeas)
* 1 medium red bell pepper
* 1.5 cups fresh flat-leaf parsley (about 1 bunch)
* 1/2 cup red onion (about 1/2 small)
* 1/2 cup celery (about 2 ribs)
* 3 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
* 3 tbsp lemon juice (from 1 to 1.5 lemons)
* 2 cloves garlic
* 1/2 tsp fine salt
* black pepper to taste
* feta cheese

## steps

Prep

1. Rinse and drain chickpeas
2. Chop red bell pepper
3. Chop fresh parsley
4. Chop red onion
5. Chop celery
6. Press or mince garlic
7. Juice lemons

Assemble

1. In a medium bowl, combine all ingredients
2. Toss until combined
3. Taste and adjust lemon juice, salt, or pepper as needed
4. Top with Feta
5. Serve immediately, or chill until ready to serve

## notes

* Leftovers keep well, covered in the refrigerator, for up to 4 days
* Can be served cold or at room temperature
* Great as a side salad, on its own, or as a meal component
