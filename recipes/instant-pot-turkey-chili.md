# Instant Pot Turkey Chili

Can double with an 8 quart instapot

## info

* 1 hour
* 6 servings

## ingredients

Aromatics and protein

* 2 tsp olive oil
* 1 lb ground turkey
* 1 small yellow onion (diced)

Broth and spices

* 1.5 cups chicken broth (or 2 cups if doubling)
* 1 tbsp soy sauce
* 1 tsp garlic powder
* 1.5 tbsp chili powder
* 2 tsp ground cumin
* 2 tsp smoked paprika
* 1 tsp dried oregano
* 1 tsp white sugar
* 1 tsp kosher salt (plus more to taste)

Cans and vegetables

* 19 oz can cannellini beans (drained and rinsed)
* 19 oz can red kidney beans (drained and rinsed)
* 14 oz can diced tomatoes
* 4 oz can chopped green chilies
* 3 tbsp tomato paste
* 1 cup frozen corn

Garnishes (optional)

* Cheddar cheese
* Green onion
* Avocado
* Sour cream
* Tortilla chips

## steps

Prep

1. Dice onion
2. Drain and rinse cannellini beans
3. Drain and rinse red kidney beans

Cook

1. Turn instant pot to saute mode and heat olive oil
2. Add ground turkey, salt, and garlic powder
3. Cook turkey until browned and cooked through, breaking up with a wooden spoon
4. Add diced onion
5. Saute until onion is softened, about 3-4 minutes
6. Press cancel to turn off saute mode
7. Add 1/2 cup chicken broth to the pot
8. Deglaze the pot bottom using a wooden spoon, scraping up all browned bits
9. Add remaining chicken broth (1 cup or 1.5 if doubled)
10. Add chili powder, ground cumin, smoked paprika, dried oregano, and white sugar
11. Layer remaining ingredients as added (do not stir)
12. Add cannellini beans and red kidney beans
13. Add diced tomatoes, chopped green chilies, tomato paste, and soy sauce
14. Add frozen corn on top
15. Pressure cook **high 10 minutes** (same cook time even if recipe doubled)
16. **Natural release 10 minutes** (or longer if you have time)
17. Quick release any remaining pressure
18. Open lid and stir all ingredients together thoroughly
19. Taste and adjust salt as needed
