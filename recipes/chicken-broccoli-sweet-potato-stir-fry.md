# Chicken & Broccoli Stir-Fry Over Sweet Potato

## info

* 45 minutes
* 2x version of the normal recipe

## ingredients

* 6 sweet potatoes (medium sized, or half a large one)
* 2 lbs skinless, bonless chicken breast (diced)
* 2 red bell peppers (diced)
* 10 cups broccoli (chopped)

sauce

* 2 tbsp ginger, minced
* 10 tbps soy sauce
* 6 tbsp coconut/palm/regular sugar
* 2 tbsp sriracha sauce
* 2 tbsp arrowroot starch (or corn starch)
* 4 tbsp water
* 2 tbsp ginger (minced)
* 2 tbsp sesame oil

## steps

Prep

1. Cook sweet potatoes at 400 F for 45 minutes after poking some holes with a fork or knife
2. Dice bell peppers
3. Cut broccoli
4. Dice chicken
5. Mince ginger
6. Prep sauce

Cook

1. Cook chicken and set said
2. Cook bell pepper for 2 minutes
3. Add broccoli and steam for 3 minutes
4. Turn up heat and add sauce until it shimmers
5. Add chicken and turn off heat

## notes

* healthy
* use less sriracha if you want less spicy
