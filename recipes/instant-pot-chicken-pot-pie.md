# Instant Pot Chicken Pot Pie

Easy comfort food recipe.

## info

* 35 minutes
* 6 servings

## ingredients

* 3 boneless chicken breasts
* 4 potatoes (russet or whatever)
* 16 oz frozen mixed vegetables
* 1/2 white onion
* 1/2 cup (chopped) celery
* 1 can (10.5 oz) cream of chicken soup
* 1 cup milk
* 1 cup chicken broth
* 16 oz canned biscuits (8 count)
* 1/2 tsp poultry seasoning
* 1 tsp garlic powder
* 1 tsp salt
* 1/2 tsp pepper

## steps

Prep

1. Peel and dice potatoes
2. Chop onion
3. Chop celery

Cook

1. Place chicken breasts, potatoes, frozen vegetables, onion, and celery in the instant pot
2. Add cream of chicken soup, milk, and chicken broth
3. Add garlic powder, poultry seasoning, salt, and pepper
4. Seal the lid and set to sealing position
5. Press manual button and select **high pressure 25 minutes**
6. While cooking, prepare biscuits according to package directions
7. **Quick release** to depressurize
9. Remove chicken and shred using two forks
9. Return shredded chicken to pot and stir to combine
10. Serve in bowls topped with a biscuit
